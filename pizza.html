Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough


    1. In a small bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and active dry yeast. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
    2. n a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and add the activated yeast mixture and olive oil. Mix until the dough comes together.
    3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours or until it doubles in size.

  2. Preheat the Oven


    Preheat your oven to the highest temperature it can go (usually around 475-500°F or 245-260°C).

  3. Roll Out the Dough


    Punch down the risen dough and knead it briefly. Roll it out on a floured surface into your desired pizza shape and thickness.

  4. Prepare the Pizza


    1. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or a baking sheet. If you have a pizza peel, you can assemble the pizza on it for easy transfer to the oven.
    2. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
    3. Add your desired toppings. Be creative!

  5. Bake the Pizza


    Carefully transfer the pizza to the preheated oven. Bake for about 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  6. Serve:


    Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. Serve and enjoy your homemade pizza!
