Ingredients

  • 2.5 cups water
  • Maggi
  • Maggi Masala
  • chopped veggies like carrot,onion,green peas

Steps

  1. Boil Water
  2. Add Maggi Noodles
  3. Cook Noodles
  4. Drain Excess Water
  5. Add Maggi Masala
  6. Mix Well
  7. Optional: Add Vegetables
  8. Serve
