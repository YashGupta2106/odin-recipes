Recipe 1

Description

Cold coffee refers to any coffee-based beverage that is served chilled or over ice. There are various methods to prepare cold coffee, and it has become a popular and refreshing alternative to traditional hot coffee, especially in warmer climates. Cold coffee can be made using different coffee brewing techniques, such as cold brew, iced coffee, or blended coffee drinks.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup coarsely ground coffee beans
  • 4 ½ cups cold water
  • cheesecloth
  • coffee filters

Steps

  1. Put coffee grounds in a large container. Slowly pour water over grounds.
  2. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and steep at room temperature for 18 to 24 hours.
  3. Line a strainer with several layers of cheesecloth and place atop a pitcher; strain coffee through the cheesecloth into the pitcher. Discard the grounds. To get a clearer brew, strain coffee again through coffee filters. Store in refrigerator.
